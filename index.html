Our Story

More than a barbershop

At Lee's Salon and Barber, we don't just cut hair. We create a space where every client feels at home. From the moment you walk in, our team greets you with personalized attention to understand exactly what you're looking for.

We offer services for men, combining classic barbering techniques with modern trends. Every cut is crafted to match your personality and lifestyle.

Located at 14130 Noblewood Plz in Woodbridge, we are the barbershop the community recommends. With a 4.2-star rating and 52+ reviews, our work speaks for itself.

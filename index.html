Tips

Grooming Insights

Practical advice from our barbers.

Haircare

How Often Should You Get a Haircut?

For most men, every 3 to 4 weeks keeps a clean look. If you have a skin fade, come in every 2 weeks to maintain crisp edges. Your barber can set the right schedule based on your hair type and style.

Grooming

How to Maintain Your Fade Between Visits

Keep the back and sides clean with a trimmer between visits. Use a good pomade or styling product to maintain shape on top. Wash your hair regularly but avoid over-washing which strips natural oils.

Tips

How to Choose the Right Hairstyle

Consider your face shape, hair texture, and lifestyle. Bring reference photos to your barber. A good barber will tell you what works with your hair type and adapt the style to fit you perfectly.
